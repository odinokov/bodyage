For informational and educational use only. Not a medical diagnosis. ABSI captures one dimension of health; it ignores muscle mass, fitness, and genetics.

Method

ABSI = waist / (BMI2/3 · height1/2). The z-score is mapped to relative mortality risk, then converted to years: Δyears = T · ln(RR) / ln(2) with sex-specific doubling time T from a Gompertz–Makeham fit to NCHS 2023 US life tables, ages 20–80 (male 8.25 yr, female 7.27 yr). The original R app used a single T ≈ 6.93 yr.

Risk values may differ by a few percent from other ABSI calculators due to the polynomial fit used.
